三种方式配置Mac OS X的启动项

# Login Items

`System Preferences -> System -> Accounts，选择Login Items选项卡`

# StartupItems

- `/System/Library/StartupItems`

- `/Library/StartupItems`

# Launchd Daemon

`~/Library/LaunchAgents`
